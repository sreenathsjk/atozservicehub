Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your information.

Information We Collect

AtoZ Service Hub collects minimal information. When you use our website, we may collect basic analytics data such as page views and device type to improve our service. If you submit a contact form, we collect only the name and email address you provide.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to improve the website experience and respond to your enquiries. We do not sell, rent or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies

We may use essential cookies to remember preferences and analyze traffic patterns. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect some website functionality.

Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to external websites such as official government portals, course platforms and job boards. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites and encourage you to review their policies separately.

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect any data you share with us. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted to us.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us and we will respond within 24 hours.
